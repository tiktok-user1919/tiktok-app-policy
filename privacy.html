Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TikTok Auto Poster collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

This application collects only the TikTok OAuth 2.0 access token necessary to authenticate and post content on your behalf. No other personal data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

The access token is used solely to post video content to your authorized TikTok account. It is not used for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers owned by this application.

4. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties under any circumstances.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us via GitHub at https://github.com/tiktok-user1919
